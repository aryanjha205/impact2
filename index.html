Ahmedabad

Ahmedabad, in western India, is the largest city in the state of Gujarat. The Sabarmati River runs through its center.

Science City Ahmedabad

Gujarat Science City is a science education and entertainment centre located in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, India.

Dariyapur Tower

Dariapur Darwaja is situated in the north side of the Ahmedabad. It was built of Ashlars Masonry in the 16th century A.D.

Takshashila Air

Takshashila Air is a residential complex in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, developed by the Takshashila Group. It is the first 25-story residential project in the city.

Sur Sagar Lake

Sursagar Lake, also known as the Chand Talao, is a lake situated in the middle of the city of Vadodara in the state of Gujarat in India.

Saputara Lake

Saputara Lake is a man-made lake in the hill station of Saputara, Gujarat, India. It's a popular spot for boating, picnics, and relaxing.

MSU Baroda

Maharaja Sayajirao University of Baroda, formerly Baroda College, is a public university in the city of Vadodara, Gujarat, India.

Galteshwar Mahadev

Galteshwar Mahadev is a Hindu temple and a popular picnic spot on the banks of the Tapi River, about 20 km from Surat, Gujarat.
